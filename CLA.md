## 8. How to Sign

Signing is done **once per contributor** in your pull request on GitHub. The CLA Assistant bot checks whether you have already signed and, if not, prompts you.

**To sign**, add a comment to your pull request with this exact text:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

The bot records your signature in [`.github/cla-signatures.json`](.github/cla-signatures.json) and marks the check as passed. You will not need to sign again for future pull requests.

By posting that comment you confirm that:
- You have read and agree to this CLA
- The contribution is your original work, or you have the right to submit it

**Entity contributors** (companies and organizations): Please send a signed copy of this Agreement to **legal@agience.ai** before your first contribution, indicating the legal entity name and the GitHub usernames of individuals authorized to contribute on its behalf. Individual contributors from your organization do not need a separate entity agreement after that.
