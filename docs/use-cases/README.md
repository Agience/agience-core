# Use cases

Status: **Reference**
Date: 2026-04-01

This section collects concrete scenarios that show what Agience enables in practice. Each use case is grounded in a realistic person with a realistic problem, and each one exercises a different slice of the platform's capabilities.

---

## Intelligent email management

Knowledge workers who receive hundreds of emails a day spend most of their time triaging noise instead of acting on signal. Agience connects to your inbox, classifies every incoming message, extracts action items, queues newsletters for a weekly digest, and drafts replies — all before you open the app. You stay in control: the platform surfaces decisions rather than making them for you, so important messages never get buried and you never send something you haven't reviewed.

## Personal assistant via Telegram

Busy people want a way to interact with their own knowledge and schedule without sitting down at a desk. Agience powers a conversational assistant reachable over Telegram: ask what is on your calendar, add items to a shopping list, capture a quick note, or create a calendar event — all by sending a text message. The assistant also monitors for urgent items and proactively surfaces conflicts or overdue tasks, so your attention goes to the right things at the right time.

## Meeting intelligence

Meetings produce commitments, decisions, and technical ideas that typically evaporate between the room and the inbox. Agience listens to a recorded meeting, segments the transcript by topic, extracts every action item and decision, generates comprehensive notes, drafts a follow-up email, and — when the conversation was technical — produces a proof-of-concept implementation based on what was discussed. By the time you close your laptop the artifacts are already in your workspace.

## Executive AI team

Startup and small-company leaders spend enormous time gathering information that already exists in their tools and assembling it into reports. Agience runs three specialized agents — an AI CFO monitoring cash flow, an AI CRO tracking pipeline health, and an AI CMO measuring campaign ROI — each delivering a focused daily or weekly report. When a board meeting approaches, a fourth agent synthesizes all three streams into a presentation draft ready for review. Board prep that once took ten hours takes forty-five minutes.

## AI-powered event planning

Planning a major event — a wedding, a corporate conference, a large birthday party — means managing a web of vendors, budgets, guest lists, and time-sensitive deadlines across dozens of communication threads. Agience initializes a dedicated workspace with a master timeline and budget allocation, then monitors email and text channels to track RSVPs automatically, flag vendor updates, and alert you the moment a category goes over budget. On the day of the event, a detailed minute-by-minute schedule with vendor contacts is ready to go.
